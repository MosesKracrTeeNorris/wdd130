The Republic of Liberia

The Republic of Liberia is located on the west coast of Africa, bordered on the south by the Atlantic Ocean, on the west by Sierra Leone, on the north by Guinea, and on the east by Ivory Coast.

The country is rich with natural mineral resources. Some of the resources include; Gold, Diamond, Iron ore, Petroleum, Forest, etc.
